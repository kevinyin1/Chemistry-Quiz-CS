Mission Statement: Ionic Four Chemistry revolves around the ideology of providing accessible, interactive, and simple tutorials for any student struggling with Chemistry.

The Ionic Four was founded on November 17th, 2016. It was created to educate the minds of chemistry students.
The founders of this organization are Wilson Guo, Kevin Yin, Jason Chen, and Danielle Naggar.
Wilson was the communication master and the co-content provider who has an extensive knowledge in chemistry.
Kevin was the firebase expert along with work in JQuery and Javascript.
Jason provided work on the web design aspect of the website.
Lastly, Danielle helped formulate the layout of each page and was the main content provider.

Together we form the Iconic Four!
